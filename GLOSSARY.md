# Concurrency Glossary (The Art of Multiprocessor Programming)

The canonical language for this workspace: terms from concurrent and
multiprocessor programming as I come to understand them. Concept READMEs and
learning records should use these terms once they land here.

A term earns a place here **only once I can use it correctly** — not when it's
first introduced. So this starts mostly empty and grows as understanding does.
When several words mean the same thing, the entry picks one and lists the rest
under _Avoid_.
